Scopelytix uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform. This policy explains how we use these technologies and your choices regarding their use.

1. Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Cookies typically contain:

  • The name of the cookie
  • A unique identifier
  • Its expiration date
  • The domain it belongs to

Cookies cannot carry viruses or install malware. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use some third-party services that may set cookies when you visit our site:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how visitors interact with our site. These cookies collect information in an anonymous form.
  • Hotjar: Provides insights into user behavior through heatmaps and session recordings. You can opt-out on Hotjar's site.
  • Cloudflare: Security and performance cookies that help protect our site and improve loading times.

These third-party cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of these providers. We have ensured that all third-party services we use comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

4. Your Cookie Choices

You have several options to manage cookies on our site:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Here's how to access cookie settings in popular browsers:

Google Chrome
Mozilla Firefox
Apple Safari

To manage cookies in your browser, look for settings like "Privacy," "Content Settings," or "Cookies."

Cookie Consent Tool

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the cookie icon in the bottom corner of any page.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our site. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the site to function properly.

5. Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other tracking technologies:

  • Web Beacons: Small graphic images that may be included on our site that allow us to measure user engagement.
  • Local Storage: Used to store information on your device to enhance site performance and remember preferences.
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage that is deleted when you close your browser.

These technologies help us improve site performance and user experience without collecting personally identifiable information.
